================================================================
IPL 2026 FANTASY CRICKET POINTS SCORING RULEBOOK
================================================================
Version: 1.0
Reference: IPL 2026 Official Fantasy Points System

----------------------------------------------------------------
GENERAL RULES
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Points are awarded to ANY player who performs the action,
  regardless of their designated role (batter, bowler,
  all-rounder, wicket-keeper). A bowler who bats earns batting
  points. A batter who bowls earns bowling points.
- There are no captain or vice-captain multipliers.
- All point categories below apply universally across roles.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1: BATTING POINTS
----------------------------------------------------------------

ACTION                          POINTS
------------------------------  -------
Run scored                        +1 per run
Boundary (4) bonus                +2
Six bonus                         +6  (i.e. +1 run + +2 boundary + +4 six bonus)
Dismissal for a duck              -2

RUN MILESTONE BONUS (only the highest milestone applies):
  30 runs scored                  +4
  50 runs scored                  +8   (do NOT also award +4 for 30)
  75 runs scored                  +12  (do NOT also award +4 or +8 for 30 or 50)
  100 runs scored                 +16  (do NOT award any lower milestone bonuses)

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- When a player reaches a milestone (30, 50, 75, or 100 runs),
  only the single highest applicable bonus is awarded. Lower
  milestone bonuses are NOT stacked or awarded alongside it.
- Overthrow runs are credited to the batter on strike at the
  time of the delivery.
- If an overthrow reaches the boundary, the batter receives the
  run points but does NOT receive an additional boundary bonus
  for those overthrow runs.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: BOWLING POINTS
----------------------------------------------------------------

ACTION                          POINTS
------------------------------  -------
Dot ball                          +1
Wicket (excluding run out)        +25
LBW or Bowled bonus               +8  (awarded in addition to the +25 wicket points)
3-wicket bonus                    +4
4-wicket bonus                    +8
5-wicket bonus                    +16
Maiden over                       +12

WICKET MILESTONE BONUS NOTES:
- Wicket milestone bonuses (3, 4, 5 wickets) are independent
  and cumulative. A bowler taking 5 wickets receives:
  +4 (3-wicket bonus) + +8 (4-wicket bonus) + +16 (5-wicket bonus).

ECONOMY RATE POINTS (minimum 2 overs bowled required):

ECONOMY RATE (runs per over)    POINTS
------------------------------  -------
Below 5.00                        +6
5.00 to 5.99                      +4
6.00 to 7.99                      +2
8.00 to 9.99                       0
10.00 to 11.00                    -2
11.01 to 12.00                    -4
Above 12.00                       -6

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: FIELDING POINTS
----------------------------------------------------------------

ACTION                          POINTS
------------------------------  -------
Catch                             +8
3-catch bonus                     +4  (flat bonus once 3 or more catches are taken;
                                       taking 4, 5, or 6 catches still gives only +4)
Stumping                          +12
Run out (direct hit)              +12
Run out (not a direct hit)        +6

RUN OUT NOTES:
- A direct hit is defined as: the fielder who executes the run
  out is the only fielder to touch the ball after the batter
  faces the delivery. The fielder receives +12.
- For a non-direct-hit run out, points are awarded to the last
  2 fielders who touched the ball. Each of those 2 fielders
  receives +6 (not split; each gets the full +6).

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: BATTING STRIKE RATE POINTS
----------------------------------------------------------------
Minimum 10 balls faced required. Applies to all players
EXCEPT bowlers who have not batted.

STRIKE RATE (runs per 100 balls)  POINTS
--------------------------------  -------
Above 170                           +6
150.01 to 170.00                    +4
130.00 to 150.00                    +2
70.01 to 129.99                      0
60.00 to 70.00                      -2
50.00 to 59.99                      -4
Below 50.00                         -6

IMPORTANT NOTE:
- Negative strike rate points apply ONLY when a player's
  individual strike rate is 70.00 or below.
- The range 70.01 to 129.99 is a neutral zone worth 0 points.
